# Andrej Karpathy second-brain pattern

**Source:** https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f

## Key principles

- Keep useful project knowledge in plain text that both humans and language models can search.
- Separate durable knowledge from raw inputs and keep navigation explicit.
- Prefer concise, linked notes over one large document.
- Make maintenance and retrieval part of the system, not an afterthought.
