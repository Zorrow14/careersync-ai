# Personas and audiences

## Candidate

Students and fresh graduates need a clearer answer to “am I ready, what is missing, and what should I do next?” The candidate portal supports profile building, explainable readiness, job/company discovery, application tracking, and AI preparation.

## Employer

Recruiters need a faster way to find relevant early-career talent and explain why a candidate fits. The employer portal supports ranked discovery, fit reports, applications, pipeline movement, and hiring analytics.

## University

Career offices need cohort-level signals rather than isolated resumes. The university portal presents employability, student, curriculum, trend, and report views with generated cohort insights.
